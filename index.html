About Me

Hi, I'm Stephanie! I'm from BC, Canada and feel happiest when I'm surrounded by mountains. After graduating from the University of British Columbia I moved to San Francisco with my partner and got into the tech scene, where I worked at a few different startups doing technical support and learned a ton about different career trajectories in technology. I soon realized web development was a natural fit for me and never looked back. When not working on a project or outside enjoying nature, I tend to be traveling and learning about different cultures around the world. Oh, and I also love sci-fi TV, home improvement projects, cooking vegetarian food, playing with my pets and going for bubble tea with my friends!
